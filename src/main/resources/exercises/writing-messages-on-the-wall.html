Writing Messages on the Wall

This is a good exercise to strengthen and work your eye muscles.
First, stand or sit facing a blank wall that is at least 8 feet away.
There shouldn't be any pictures or windows on the wall.
Next, imagine that your eyes are lasers, just like a superpower!
Then use them to write words on the wall.
Trace all the words with your laser eyes.
This exercise will make your eye muscles move very fast in a variety of directions.
As a result, your weakest eye muscles will get stimulation and a great workout.
Write words with your eyes for about 20 seconds and then take a small rest.
Repeat for a total of 2 minutes for best results.
